3
HOW THE SIGNAL ENGINE WORKS
QUANTRA BEAST runs 8 independent analysis engines simultaneously. Each engine looks at a different aspect of the market. They vote, and the composite score decides the signal.
📈 TREND
Checks EMA 9/21/50. Is price above or below its moving averages? Short-term vs long-term direction.
⚡ VOLATILITY
Reads India VIX. High VIX = fear/big moves expected. Low VIX = calm market, options are cheap.
📊 OPTIONS
Reads PCR (Put-Call Ratio) and Open Interest. Where are the big players placing bets?
🎯 GAMMA
Gamma exposure near ATM. High gamma = market makers force big moves. Amplifies signals.
🔄 REGIME
Is the market trending or ranging? RSI, Bollinger Bands, ATR. Is momentum building or fading?
💰 FLOW
FII/DII activity. Are foreign investors buying or selling? Smart money flow direction.
🧠 SENTIMENT
Max Pain, support/resistance from OI buildup. Where will market gravitate by expiry?
📰 NEWS
Live market news scored for impact. RBI announcements, FII data, global cues — auto-analyzed.
✅ Does it analyse charts? Yes — TREND and REGIME engines analyse price action using EMA crossovers, RSI momentum, MACD, and Bollinger Band breakouts. It's the equivalent of reading 3 timeframe charts simultaneously. The Entry Timing Score specifically waits for all chart conditions to align before recommending entry.
4
READING THE SIGNAL — WHAT DO NUMBERS MEAN?
SIGNAL CARD
BUY CE (CALL) = Market going UP → Buy a Call option
BUY PE (PUT) = Market going DOWN → Buy a Put option
WAIT / HOLD = Mixed signals → See Straddle recommendation
Confidence % = How strongly the engines agree (60%+ = trade)
Composite score = Sum of all engine votes (-100 to +100)
ENTRY TIMING SCORE (most important!)
0–39: Do NOT enter. Wait.
40–59: Getting ready. Watch closely.
60–79: ✅ Good entry. Place trade now.
80–100: 🚀 Strong setup. High confidence.
The score combines chart momentum + options flow + timing. Never enter below 60.
5
MIXED SIGNAL → STRADDLE / STRANGLE STRATEGY
When engines are split (e.g. 4 bull + 4 bear), the dashboard shows a ⚖️ LONG STRADDLE or 🦅 LONG STRANGLE recommendation instead of WAIT. This means: the market is coiled and will make a big move — we just don't know which direction yet.
⚖️ LONG STRADDLE
Buy ATM CE + ATM PE at the same strike. Best when VIX is low (cheap premiums). Profits from any big move — up or down.

Example: Nifty at 22500 → Buy 22500 CE + Buy 22500 PE
🦅 LONG STRANGLE
Buy OTM CE + OTM PE at different strikes. Cheaper cost but needs bigger move to profit. Shown when engine picks slightly different strikes.

Example: Nifty at 22500 → Buy 22600 CE + Buy 22400 PE
HOW TO MANAGE A STRADDLE TRADE
1. Buy both legs simultaneously (CE + PE) when market opens
2. Watch which side starts moving — that leg gains value quickly
3. Book the winning leg when it gains 80–100% profit
4. Exit the losing leg at 30–40% loss (it won't recover)
5. Net result: winning leg profits > losing leg loss = overall profit
6. Avoid holding past 3:00 PM — theta decay (time decay) eats value fast
⚠️ When to AVOID straddle: If VIX is above 20 (premiums too expensive), if expiry is same day (theta kills you), or if there's no expected event/move. The dashboard will show AVOID automatically when these conditions are met.

④ Go to Kite → Search that symbol → Buy
⑤ Come back → click CONFIRM ENTRY
⑥ Dashboard starts live monitoring your P&L
⑦ Exit when target hit OR stop loss breached
WHERE TO FIND OPTION PRICES IN KITE
1. Open kite.zerodha.com
2. Search the Zerodha symbol shown here

3. The market price = option premium (₹)
4. This ₹ price is what the dashboard calls "Entry"
5. For real-time prices also check NSE Options Chain
   at nseindia.com → Market Data → Options Chain
⭐ GOLDEN RULES — NEVER BREAK THESE
❌ Never trade against both PCR + VIX combo signals
❌ Never enter if Entry Timing Score is below 60
❌ Never risk more than 2% of capital per trade
❌ Stop trading after 3 losses in a day — review tomorrow
✅ Always set stop loss before entering
✅ Scale out 50% of position at 1R (1× risk earned)
✅ Let the system run — don't override signals emotionally
✅ Market open 9:20–10:00 AM = best entry window
